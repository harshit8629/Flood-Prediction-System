🛡️ Flood Safety Tips

✅ Before a Flood

1. Keep an emergency kit ready with food, water and medicines.

2. Know your nearest evacuation route and shelter location.

3. Keep all important documents in a waterproof bag.

4. Charge all devices and keep a power bank ready.

5. Store at least 3 days of drinking water.

⚠️ During a Flood

1. Move immediately to higher ground.

2. Do not walk or drive through flood water.

3. Disconnect all electrical appliances.

4. Stay away from rivers, drains and bridges.

5. Listen to official announcements only.

🔄 After a Flood

1. Do not return home until authorities say it is safe.

2. Check for structural damage before entering buildings.

3. Drink only boiled or bottled water.

4. Watch out for snakes and insects in flooded areas.

5. Report damage to local authorities immediately.
