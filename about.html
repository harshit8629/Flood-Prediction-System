About This Project

The Flood Prediction System is a web-based application that helps predict flood risk based on real-time environmental data such as rainfall, river water levels, humidity and temperature.


How It Works

Users enter current environmental data for their area. Our rule-based prediction engine analyses the data and calculates whether the flood risk is Low, Medium or High. The system then provides safety suggestions and emergency contact information.
